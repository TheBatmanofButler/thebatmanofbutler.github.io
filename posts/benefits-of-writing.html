Benefits of writing

November 2, 2020

Most of the time, writing is a chore for me. I want to try to change that because I think writing can be a useful tool to organize my thoughts and decisions more clearly.

It feels intimidating to write publicly because I feel compelled to write a lot, with great meaning. But I think that one of the best benefits of writing is that I can read what I wrote and trim it to have more precision. So maybe less is more.
